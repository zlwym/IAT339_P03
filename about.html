Resume

Technology-obsessed designer, researcher, and programmer voted most talkative and most likely to be found in the outdoors.

Originally from the world of health sciences, I study interactive arts and technology at Simon Fraser University and previously at Queensland University of Technology. My journey from the sciences to design and technology have instilled in me a passion for accessibility, sustainability, and inclusion and equity.

My data and evidence driven work is influenced by my love of research and curiosity about the world. As a senior student, I love collaborating with diverse stakeholders, learning from a variety of perspectives, and teaching junior interactive arts and technology students.

From strategizing disease prevention and striving for a perfect titration, I now obsess over pixel-perfect placement and dissecting data. When I’m not designing, programming, or researching, you can find me discovering new music, hiking through the mountains, or making a mess with paint.
