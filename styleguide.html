Interactive Elements

In-text link

CSS selector:

.in-text-link

Sample code:

<p>Link to <a href = "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dog">dogs</a>!</p>

Rendered element:

Link to DOGS!

Navigation Link

CSS selector:

.button-nav

Sample code:

<a href="#" class="button">Default Button</a>

Rendered element:

Default Button

Submit/Reset Buttons

CSS selector:

input[type=submit]

input[type=reset]

Sample code:

<input type="submit" name="submit" value="submit"> <input type="reset" name="reset" value="reset">

Rendered element:

Text Input Field with Label

CSS selector:

.input-field

Sample code:

<label for="form-input-field">Label</label> <input type="text" placeholder="input" id="form-input-field">

Rendered element:

Radio Button with Label

CSS selector:

.radio

Sample code:

<input class="radio-input" type="radio" name="button" value="sandra" id="button1"> <label class="radio-label" for="button1">Sandra</label>

Rendered element:

Image as a Link

CSS selector:

.img-container

Sample code:

<div class="img-container"> <a href="mosaic.html"> <img class="image-link" src="img/mosaic_spash_screen_green.png" alt="mosaic"> </a> </div>

Paragraphs

CSS Selector:

p

Sample code:

<p>This is a paragraph about dogs. Dogs are a popular pet, earning them the title "man's best friend". I have a dog and many of my friends and family have dogs. They come in a variety of breeds, colours, and sizes. Each type of dog has unique characteristics, such as heightened sense of smell or good stamina. Dogs have been a notable animal since early human history, appearing in ancient hieroglyphics and art pieces.</p>

Rendered element:

This is a paragraph about dogs. Dogs are a popular pet, earning them the title "man's best friend". I have a dog and many of my friends and family have dogs. They come in a variety of breeds, colours, and sizes. Each type of dog has unique characteristics, such as heightened sense of smell or good stamina. Dogs have been a notable animal since early human history, appearing in ancient hieroglyphics and art pieces.
